TL;DR: 双层石墨烯在强垂直电场下出现独特的输运现象，包括电阻峰分裂、霍尔电阻符号反转和滞后效应，揭示了带隙内束缚态的重要物理机制。

Motivation: 传统双栅晶体管受限于介质击穿，只能研究Δ≪t_⊥（约360 meV）的弱电场区域，无法探索Δ>t_⊥的强电场物理。本文旨在通过双离子栅技术突破这一限制，研究强垂直电场下双层石墨烯的独特电子性质。

Method: 采用双离子栅技术实现超过1 V/nm的强电场，使层间势能差Δ>t_⊥。通过测量纵向电阻R_xx和霍尔电阻随栅压的变化，观察输运特性的演化，特别是Δ≈t_⊥时的"膝盖"特征和Δ>t_⊥时的异常行为。

Result: 在Δ≈t_⊥时，R_xx峰随栅压的演化出现明显的"膝盖"转折点。当Δ>t_⊥时，R_xx峰幅值减小并出现分裂，霍尔电阻多次符号反转，峰位置出现滞后效应。这些现象归因于带隙内束缚态能量随电场变化，在足够大的Δ下穿过中隙能级。

Conclusion: 双离子栅技术成功实现了双层石墨烯强电场区域的研究，揭示了带隙内束缚态的独特物理性质。这些束缚态能量的变化显著影响电子态密度和化学势演化，为理解双层石墨烯的电子结构提供了新视角。

Abstract: The band structure of Bernal-stacked bilayer graphene can be tuned using double-gated transistors to apply a perpendicular electric field that generates an interlayer potential energy difference $Δ$. Dielectric breakdown limits the operation of conventional devices to the $Δ\ll t_\perp \simeq 360$ meV regime. We employ double ionic gating to reach fields past $ 1$ V/nm, for which $Δ> t_\perp$. We find that for $Δ\simeq t_\perp$, the evolution of the longitudinal resistance ($R_{xx}$) peak as a function of applied gate voltages undergoes a sharp change in slope, exhibiting a pronounced "knee". Increasing $Δ$ past the "knee" results in an unusual evolution transport properties: the peak in $R_{xx}$ decreases in magnitude, it exhibits a splitting concomitant with multiple sign reversals of the Hall resistance, and hysteresis in the peak position emerges. We explain the observed phenomenology in terms of in-gap bound states, whose energy strongly depends on the perpendicular electric field, and crosses the mid-gap level for sufficiently large $Δ> t_\perp$. The phenomenon causes large changes in the electronic density of in-gap states that profoundly affect the evolution of the chemical potential. Our experimental results and their interpretation reveal unique aspects of the physics of in-gap states in Bernal bilayer graphene and demonstrate that double ionic gating enables investigating the large-$Δ$ regime, which has remained experimentally inaccessible so far.
